In the heart of the city,
a plastic sheet drapes over a building under construction.
Beyond it, the faint outline of a structure emerges,

nearing completion yet still in flux.
Beta Space represents this experimental stage
just before the final version.
Within it, we continue to shape ourselves in our own ways.

Through time filled with thought, trial, failure, and repetition,
each graduating student has sought their own form.
This exhibition highlights the process, not just the outcome.

Though Beta Space is not yet complete,
it is a site of creation fueled by endless possibility
and a reflection of our commitment to continuous experimentation.
